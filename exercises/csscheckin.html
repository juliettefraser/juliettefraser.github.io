Blueberry Muffin Recipe!

Satisfy those cravings with this easy yet delicious blueberry muffin recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint of blueberries (frozen or fresh)
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and add cupcake liners to a muffin pan.
  2. Mix together dry ingredients: flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. In a different bowl whisk the egg, butter, and vanilla. Add these ingredients into the large bowl with the dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  4. Fold in the blueberries to the dough.
  5. Fill muffin cups with the blended batter.
  6. Bake muffins for about 20-25 minutes.
  7. Let the muffins cool before taking them out of the pan.
