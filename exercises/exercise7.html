Writing Samples

Microsoft Word Manual (PDF)
This 24 page Microsoft Word manual geared towards book editors was a semester-long project for my Professional Writing and Technical Communications class. The final product was printed in color and spiral bound.

Faux Grant Proposal (PDF)
This 26 page faux grant proposal was written for my Introduction to Professional Writing class. It is composed on the behalf of the non-profit organization: Second Chance Animal Services and addresses the critical issues of limited access to veterinary care, pet food insecurity, animal overpopulation, and financial constraints. Pieces of this project were created with the aid of AI technology.

American Studies Final Paper (PDF)
This 10 page final paper for my American Studies class addresses the themes of belonging and unconformity in the two novels, How Much of These Hills is Gold and Outlawed. The paper also examines the novels relation to the essay on “Society” from Keywords for American Cultural Studies.

Roles of Conflict in Literature Research Paper (PDF)
This 8 page research paper for my junior year English class explores the two novels, Cal and Mother to Mother as they allegorize and problematize their characters into Christ figures in order to reveal a deeper understanding of conflict in literature and in the real world.
