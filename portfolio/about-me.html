A little about me

I am a third-year student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, majoring in English with a minor in Information Technology. Alongside my studies, I am pursuing a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communications and a specialization in The Study and Practice of Writing. After graduation, I plan to delve into the field of technical writing.

As an English major and IT minor, I am eager to expand my skills in a field that blends language and technology. I have always been an avid book reader and have always loved writing creatively, but it was not until I started college that I found technical writing. I am excited to explore this field to help bridge the gap between complex concepts and clear, understandable language. Outside of school, I love to go for walks with my dog Molly, and spend time with friends.
